Our Impact

All proceeds from the Run for Chief race are donated to Jaden's Fund at Cincinnati Children's Hospital! This fund provides support for heart transplant patients as well as their families.

  • An all-inclusive day at Kings Island for patients and their families
  • Christmas gifts for the patients and their siblings
  • Sundaes on Sundays
  • Lunches for the doctors, nurses, and support staff who provide world-class care

We are incredibly grateful to everyone who has supported our mission over the past two years to celebrate Jay Chief and to help the children who find themselves in a similar situation that Chief did.
